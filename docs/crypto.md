# Crypto assets
In Germany, cryptocurrencies are not capital assets. 
They are "Private Economic Assets" (Privates Veräußerungsgeschäft).

The following taxation applies:

| Asset Held          | Timeframe   | Tax Rate                       |
|:--------------------|:------------|:-------------------------------|
| **Crypto**          | < 12 Months | Personal Income Rate (14%–45%) |
| **Crypto**          | > 12 Months | **0% (Tax-Free)**              |
| **Staking Rewards** | On Receipt  | Personal Income Rate (14%–45%) |
