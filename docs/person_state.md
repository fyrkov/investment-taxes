# Person State

Investment tax calculation in Germany is influenced by a combination of 
personal attributes and tax-relevant settings

1. **Tax Residency**
   Whether the person is a tax resident in Germany and/or other jurisdictions.

2. **Church Tax (Kirchensteuer) Affiliation**
   - Applies only if a person is officially registered as a member of a recognized church.
   - If not registered, no church tax is due.
   - If registered, the tax is calculated as a percentage of the income tax (usually 8% or 9%).

3. **Exemption Order (FSA) Status**
   The status of the tax-free allowance (*Sparer-Pauschbetrag*).
   See [Freistellungsauftrag (FSA), Sparer-Pauschbetrag, Exemption Order](fsa.md)

4. **Non-Assessment (NV) Certificates**
   - Issued by tax authorities (*Nichtveranlagungsbescheinigung*).
   - Confirms that a person is not subject to income tax because their total income is below the taxable threshold.

   Relation to US taxes, for example, status as a "US Person."
